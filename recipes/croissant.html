The Fluffiest Croissant

Description

An absolute staple in my girlfriend's breakfast. If her croissant isn't good then her morning isn't good. To keep her happy, I use the following recipe.

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Kosher Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Make the dough with flour, sugar, yeast, salt, butter, and milk.
  2. Chill the dough.
  3. Roll and fold the dough 4-6 times.
  4. Re-Chill the dough.
  5. Roll out the dough to an appropriate thickness.
  6. Cut the dough into long skinny triangles.
  7. Roll the triangles into a crescent shape.
  8. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise
  9. Bake
